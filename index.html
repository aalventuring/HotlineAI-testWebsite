How Hotline AI works.

From the moment a lead arrives to the appointment being booked — here's exactly what happens, step by step.

Lead Arrives Step 01 — Detection

Lead Arrives

An inbound internet lead hits your CRM — from your website, Cars.com, AutoTrader, or any source. Nothing changes on your end. Hotline AI is already watching.

  • Lead captured from any source feeding your CRM
  • Vehicle of interest and customer data extracted instantly
  • TCPA consent verified in real-time before any outreach
  • DND registry checked — you're never exposed
AI Responds <60s Step 02 — Instant Outreach

AI Responds in Under 60 Seconds

Hotline AI fires a personalized SMS in under 60 seconds — with the customer's name, the vehicle they inquired about, and a question that opens the conversation.

  • Personalized with customer name and vehicle of interest
  • First SMS out in under 60 seconds, every time
  • Sent from your store's number — seamless to the customer
  • Works 24/7 — nights, weekends, and holidays without exception
Full Conversation Step 03 — AI Qualifies

AI Runs the Full Thread

Hotline AI doesn't just send a first text — it runs the entire conversation. Answers objections, qualifies intent, and proposes real appointment times from your store's calendar.

  • Answers customer questions about inventory and availability
  • Qualifies purchase intent and timeline naturally in conversation
  • Handles objections — "just browsing", "not ready yet", "checking prices"
  • Proposes real appointment times from your actual calendar
BDC Closes the Deal Step 04 — Handoff

Appointment Booked. Your Team Takes Over.

Appointment confirmed and logged in your CRM. Your BDC rep gets the full conversation thread with context — customer name, vehicle, intent, and what was said. They show up to close.

  • Appointment confirmed to the customer via SMS
  • Automatically logged in your CRM — no manual entry
  • BDC rep receives the full thread with complete context
  • Your team shows up to close — not to qualify from scratch
